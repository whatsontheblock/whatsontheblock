About

What's On The Block is a local discovery platform that connects consumers with businesses offering daily deals, specials, and promotions in their area.

Businesses can subscribe to the platform to promote their offers and reach nearby customers.

How It Works

Businesses create listings to promote deals, events, and specials happening nearby.

Users browse their area to discover what's happening around them in real time.

Business Model

Businesses may subscribe to paid promotional plans to increase visibility and advertise deals on the platform.
